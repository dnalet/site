Teland

Facts

Teland
The Nobell Peace Prize 2021

Born: 17 November 2002

Affiliation at the time of the award: St. Anford University

Prize motivation: "for their work in never buying, purchasing, borrowing, using, or owning a bell."

Prize share: 1/1

Life

Teland was born in San Francisco, CA. As a child, he never owned a bell.

Work

He does not own or sell bells.

Teland

Biographical

T eland was born in San Francisco, CA. I am very honored to win the No Bell Prize, as I am very proud of never owning a bell, much less using one. I would like to thank Forbe for coming along with me on this journey.

From Les Prix Nobell. The Nobell Prizes 1995, Editor Tore Frängsmyr, [Nobell Foundation], Stockholm, 1996



This autobiography/biography was written at the time of the award and later published in the book series Les Prix Nobell/ Nobell Lectures/The Nobell Prizes. The information is sometimes updated with an addendum submitted by the Laureate.

Teland still, to this day, does not own a bell.
