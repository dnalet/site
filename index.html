Background

Hi! My name is Teland and I am from the Bay Area, California. This is my personal site where I will post personal projects and random stuff.

I was previously the head of growth at Bumper Investing I'm a student at Stanford University in Palo Alto. I was previously a Product Manager for the Stanford Daily, the university's newspaper, and a marketing analyst for Rippling, a PEO provider.

Projects

I have a lot of hobbies that I like to work on. Here are some of the things that I kinda like to do.

Games

Making games is a hobby of mine - one of my games became the most popular game on Twitch, where streamers play games live. It actually received over 80,000 viewers! You can find the game here. At its peak, it received more than 40,000 users.

Photography

I like photography because it allows me to capture a moment in time. My work has been featured in SF Gate[2]. I also was an employee for the City of Daly City[3], where I would take pictures at various events to be used across social media channels.

Podcast

I host a podcast called the "Major Jobs Podcast"[4]. You can find it here. Some of my favorite guests have included Ernie Johnson, Portugal. the Man, the voice of Siri, Jeff Kaplan, and Ken Bone.

Videography

In addition to photos, I also edited videos for the City of Daly City. I use Final Cut Pro X to edit, however, as of recently, I am also using Adobe Premiere Pro as well.

Achievements

You may have seen above the "achievements" that I listed. Those are just things I do for fun that are technically true. I created forbe.org[5] as a parody to the Forbes 30 under 30 list, except I made Forbe's 30 under 30 list (note the apostrophe). I also created acrossallplatforms.com[6] because I always saw people listing their followings from "across all platforms", so I made a website called "Across All Platforms" and gave myself a large following.
